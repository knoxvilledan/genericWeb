Section TAG

Section tag is used when requirements of two headers or footer or any other sections needed. Creates standalone sections on a web page. The section tag divides content into chapters, headers, or footers.

Span TAG

Span tag wrap sections of text for styling purposes or to add attributes to a section of text. the span tag is used for inline organization and styling. The span tag is a generic inline container element.

Div TAG

The div tag is used to make divisions of content in the web page. It's a generic flow container. You can style them to look different from other sections on the page, or to position them differently. Divides content into blocks on a web page. The div tag is used for block-level organization and styling of page elements.
